Reflections

1. I learned that people usually expect to see their response up at the top once they've submitted theirs

2. I learned that most people are viewing in a smaller display size so I should focus my designs accordingly

3. Most people seemed to enjoy the interface otherwise and had no other complaits or comments

Future Updates

Based on this feedback, I hope to include some more obvious indications of interaction for cards and figure out a way so that users can see their response as soon as they submit. I also want to include more breakpoints and try to make the two-column card layout on smaller displays.
